Crime Data Analysis


Exploring the in-depth analysis of Crime Data for the year 2017, uncover patterns, and gain valuable insights into common offenses, crime trends, and peak activity periods. Visualize the data, get informative reports, and discover opportunities to enhance crime prevention strategies.


Key Features of the Project:

    Thorough analysis of Crime data for the year 2017.
    Answering important questions to extract valuable insights.
    Visualizing the data to uncover patterns and trends.
    Providing meaningful interpretations and recommendations based on the analysis.

Why Crime Data Analysis is Important:

Crime data analysis plays a crucial role in understanding patterns of criminal activity, identifying high-risk areas, and aiding in the formulation of effective law enforcement strategies. By analyzing crime data, we can gain insights into the most common offenses, peak times of criminal activity, and other valuable information that can help improve public safety and allocate resources efficiently.

Analysis Questions:

    1. What are the most common crimes in terms of offense groups?
    2. What are the least common offense groups?
    3. What are the most common offense descriptions?
    4. What is the bottom or least frequent offense description?
    5. In which year were the most crimes committed?
    6. Are there more crimes committed on specific days?
    7. Are there more crimes during specific hours?
    8. On what days and during which hours are the most crimes committed?

By answering these questions, the project aims to provide insights into crime patterns, enable proactive law enforcement strategies, and promote community safety.
